An Announcement Regarding Bobtail-Bear

Posted on 2008-06-14 Edited on 2026-06-15 In Developer Life

As some of you might have noticed, the official website of Bobtail-Bear (bobtail-bear.cn) now redirects directly to Google Code. Due to constraints on my time and energy, I have no choice but to put Bobtail-Bear on the backburner for now. I’m about to graduate from my wonderful university and kickstart my professional career, so I’m officially announcing that Bobtail-Bear is being archived indefinitely. If anyone is interested in continuing its development, feel free to hit me up so we can break the seal, haha!~

SVN Repositories:

  • UTF-8 Encoding: http://bobtail-bear.googlecode.com/svn/trunk/utf-8
  • GB2312 Encoding: http://bobtail-bear.googlecode.com/svn/trunk/gb2312

Regarding the Bobtail-Bear Free License Issues

Posted on 2007-11-27 Edited on 2026-06-15 In Developer Life

Due to the shutdown of the Bobtail-Bear official website, many users have recently been unable to log in to look up their license IDs or request new free licenses.

Here is a brief explanation: the official site went offline because the Ministry of Information Industry’s ICP filing (Bei’an) system underwent an upgrade, which unfortunately caused our existing approved filing records to get lost. Consequently, we are currently in the process of re-applying for our ICP filing. Because we cannot reuse the original filing materials, the new application process has been quite a bumpy road.

As soon as the website’s filing is completed, normal functions will be restored. Please bear with us and stay patient; we will resolve all pending issues as soon as the site is back online. In the meantime, new users can use Bobtail-Bear for free without needing to apply for a license ID for now.
